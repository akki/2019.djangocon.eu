Copenhagen, Denmark
April 10-12th 2019

After a lot of planning, we are happy to announce that we'll be hosting all you lovely Django people next year!

You don't necessarily need to buy plane tickets or book hotel rooms yet: Alternatives such as ride sharing and accommodation sharing will be provided through the website for interested ticket-holders from December and onward.


Here's our plan:

  • October

    Early organizing of the conference (volunteers, get in touch!)

  • November

    More organizing, website is booted with a Call for Participation

  • December

    Ticket sale opens, including info on scholarships

  • January

    Early bird/Sharing Pioneer discount expires

  • February

    Call for Participation closes

  • March

    Talks start being published

  • April

    Main conference (April 10-12th), sprints (April 13-14th) and opportunities to do workshops before and after
